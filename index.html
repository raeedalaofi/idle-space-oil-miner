This Privacy Policy describes how Idle Space Oil Miner (“the App”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Android mobile game published under the package name com.idlespaceoilminer.game.

By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, the following data may be processed:

  • Game progress — saved locally on your device (cash, gems, wells, settings).
  • Advertising data — ad identifiers, device type, IP address, and interaction with ads (via our ad partners).
  • Leaderboard data — anonymous player ID and scores submitted to Unity Gaming Services.
  • Purchase data — if you make in-app purchases, Google Play processes payment; we receive purchase confirmation, not your full payment card details.
  • Technical data — OS version, app version, and diagnostics needed to run the App.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information under their own policies:

Service Purpose Policy
Google AdMob (via Unity LevelPlay) Banner and rewarded ads Google Privacy Policy
Unity LevelPlay Ad mediation Unity Privacy Policy
Unity Gaming Services Anonymous sign-in & leaderboards UGS Privacy Policy
Google Play App distribution & in-app purchases Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and save your game progress
  • Display advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Operate global and seasonal leaderboards
  • Process in-app purchases and premium features
  • Improve stability and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising & Your Choices

The App shows banner and rewarded video ads. You can remove ads through in-app purchases where available. You may reset your advertising ID or limit ad personalization in your Android device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland may have additional choices regarding personalized ads under applicable law. Ad partners may show a consent dialog where required.

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • Game saves are stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
  • Leaderboard scores are retained on Unity servers while the service is active.
  • Ad partners retain data according to their own retention schedules.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To delete local game data, uninstall the App or clear its storage in Android settings. For leaderboard or service-related requests, contact us using the details below.

8. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. International Transfers

Third-party providers may process data in countries other than your own. They use appropriate safeguards as described in their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
